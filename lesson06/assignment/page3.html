Language

Snowmen at Work by Caralyn Buehner is a language rich winter and snowman themed story. This resource targets describing vocabulary, inference clues, comparing & contrasting, sorting winter and summer clothing, sequencing, story retelling, plural nouns, social skills, phonological/phonemic awareness (compound words), and comprehension questions. Step-by-step lesson directions are provided. This book companion can be differentiated for a variety of elementary grade levels. Digital Slide activities are included to lead discussions about the story AND to play various drag & drop games.
Literacy

This back to school themed literacy resource contains two vocabulary based book companions for the books Clark the Shark and The Name Jar. Follow the six step, scripted vocabulary lesson activities to engage students in discussing Tier 2 vocabulary words. Reading response worksheets are included with visual symbols to support comprehension.
