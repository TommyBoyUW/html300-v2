Spring Articulation Printable Activities for Early Sounds in Speech Therapy

352 Articulation Spring Raindrop Cards are included to target /p, b, t, d, m, n, k, g, f, v/ in all positions of words, /h/ and /w/ in the initial position of words, and CVC words in speech therapy. Students will enjoy adding raindrop picture cards to their umbrella, watering can, and/or cloud worksheets. Cut & glue worksheets for sorting sounds are also included for each speech sound. Color and black & white versions of each page are included. This is a great resource for spring themed articulation activities in speech therapy!

Visual Supports for Describing and WH Questions Bundle for Speech & Language

Target describing vocabulary words by naming six to seven attributes and answering WH Questions (who, what, where, when, why) using these visual prompts in speech and language therapy. Learners can use these pictures to increase the number of characteristics or details in their vocabulary descriptions and to more confidently answer "who, what, where, when, and why" questions. This will help target vocabulary skills and answering WH questions during speech and language activities. The Describing Vocabulary resources includes digital slides of each page with drag & drop pieces are included. This is a must-have, year round bundle for any SLP, teacher, and parent.
